## 📋 **Available Models (Reserved for Future Use)**

### **AlphaTrader**
- **Strategy**: Momentum + Trend Following
- **Description**: Identifies strong momentum trends and rides them with tight risk management
- **Strengths**:
  - Fast execution
  - Trend identification
  - High win rate in trending markets
- **Risk Profile**: Conservative
- **Typical Win Rate**: 68-72%
- **Best For**: Strong trending markets

### **QuantumAI**
- **Strategy**: Statistical Arbitrage
- **Description**: Uses statistical models to identify price discrepancies and mean reversion opportunities
- **Strengths**:
  - High frequency trading
  - Market neutral strategies
  - Low correlation to market direction
- **Risk Profile**: Low-Moderate
- **Typical Win Rate**: 70-75%
- **Best For**: Range-bound markets, pairs trading

### **NeuralNet-V2**
- **Strategy**: Deep Learning + Pattern Recognition
- **Description**: Neural network trained on historical price patterns, order flow, and market microstructure
- **Strengths**:
  - Complex pattern recognition
  - Adapts to changing market regimes
  - Multi-timeframe analysis
- **Risk Profile**: Moderate
- **Typical Win Rate**: 64-70%
- **Best For**: All market conditions

### **DeepMarket**
- **Strategy**: Reinforcement Learning
- **Description**: Self-improving model that learns optimal trading strategies through trial and error
- **Strengths**:
  - Continuous learning
  - Risk-adjusted returns
  - Portfolio optimization
- **Risk Profile**: Moderate-High
- **Typical Win Rate**: 72-78%
- **Best For**: Experienced traders, larger capital

### **CryptoSage**
- **Strategy**: Sentiment Analysis + News Trading
- **Description**: Analyzes social media, news, and on-chain data to predict market movements
- **Strengths**:
  - Early trend detection
  - Fundamental analysis integration
  - Event-driven trading
- **Risk Profile**: High
- **Typical Win Rate**: 60-68%
- **Best For**: Event-driven opportunities, news catalysts

## 🔧 **Model Configuration Guidelines**

### **Risk Management Parameters**
- **Stop Loss**: 2-5% default (configurable per model)
- **Take Profit**: 5-15% default (configurable per model)
- **Max Position Size**: 10-30% of capital
- **Max Drawdown Limit**: 20%
- **Leverage**: 1-10x (depends on model and market conditions)

### **Performance Metrics**
- **Win Rate**: % of profitable trades
- **Sharpe Ratio**: Risk-adjusted returns (>2.0 is excellent)
- **Max Drawdown**: Largest peak-to-trough decline
- **Profit Factor**: Gross profit / Gross loss (>1.5 is good)
- **Average Trade Duration**: Time held per position

### **Asset Support**
All models support:
- BTC/USDT
- ETH/USDT
- SOL/USDT
- BNB/USDT
- DOGE/USDT
- XRP/USDT

## 🚀 **Deployment Notes**

### **Single Model Deployment**
Currently running: **DeepSeek R1** with $100 starting capital

### **Multi-Model Deployment (Future)**
When deploying multiple models:
1. Allocate capital proportionally
2. Set different risk parameters per model
3. Monitor correlation between models
4. Rebalance quarterly based on performance

### **Backtesting Requirements**
Before activating any model:
- Minimum 6 months historical data
- Test across different market conditions
- Validate risk management rules
- Ensure positive Sharpe ratio (>1.5)
